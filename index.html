Directions

You can drag with touch or mouse or keyboard:

  • Shift + key: clockwise rotation
  • No Shift + key: counterclockwise rotation
  • R: right face
  • L: left face
  • U: up face
  • D: down face
  • F: front face
  • B: back face
  • M: middle vertical slice
  • E: middle horizontal slice
  • S: middle side vertical slice
  • X: move whole cube around R/L centers
  • Y: move whole cube around U/D centers
  • Z: move whole cube around F/B centers

Tested with Chrome.
